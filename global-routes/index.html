Introduction

The idea behind this page is to explore world dynamics through commonly used public transit routes. Simply sitting in a public bus and absorbing the scenes that pass by can reveal a lot about a place. The idea is to create short, visual and interactive articles that help share my observations and see a ground perspective of some of the most interspting places on earth.
